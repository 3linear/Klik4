Is PMS or custom color-matching available through Klik 4?

Absolutely. Just specify the PMS (Pantone Matching System) number(s) in the PMS/Custom Color Matching box when customizing your item.

  • Background Colors: The background color affects the way your imprint color appears; the truest color matches will be obtained when imprinting on a white item.
  • DecorationMethods: Please be aware that exact PMS matching is not possible with some imprint methods due to the nature of the imprinting process. We can usually offer ?as close as possible? matching in these instances; just let us know what color you have in mind!

If you have concerns regarding the appearance of your specified imprint color on a particular item, please call 01/4002233 or LiveChat between 9 AM and 5 PM ET Monday through Friday to speak with a promotions expert.

What is a virtual proof?

A virtual proof is a digital representation of your artwork on the product you?ve purchased. Because Klik 4 uses templates provided by the manufacturer, your virtual proof is the closest approximation to how the final product will appear.

We will not submit an order to production until you have approved your virtual proof.

  • Virtual proofs are prepared within one business day after your order is placed.
  • We will email you when your virtual proof is ready. You can then view, approve, or request changes to it.
  • After your virtual proof has been created, we are happy to revise it as needed until you are wholly satisfied.
  • Because of the labor-intensive nature of this process, we require that you submit payment before we can create a virtual proof.
  • Want to see a sample product with your logo on it? Consider ordering a spec sample, also known as a pre-production proof.

If you have any questions about your virtual proof, please call 01/4002233 or LiveChat between 9 AM and 5 PM ET Monday through Friday to speak with a promotions expert.

How do I send Klik4 my artwork?

After you've choosed &Yes, I will Upload Art& you can upload your artwork to Klik4.

  • Large Files (over 25 MB): Please contact us to request our large file upload link.
  • Preferred Formats: While we accept art in any format, we prefer black-and-white vector/print-ready artwork created in one of the following programs: Adobe Illustrator, Corel Draw, or Macromedia Freehand. Popular vector format file extensions include: .ai, .cdr, and .eps. Please avoid sending Microsoft Office document files such as Word or Excel. If an Office document is sent with images, please send them separately.
  • Vector Art Conversion: An artist will contact you to resolve any issues that arise in creating print-ready artwork if you send artwork in the following formats: .jpg, .gif, .bmp, .tif, or a Photoshop-generated .eps file. Providing non-vector artwork may delay your order, as an artist will have to retouch or recreate your artwork. Due to the labor involved in converting raster images to vector artwork, your order will be subject to an Art Conversion Fee if we have to convert your artwork to vector format.
  • Troubleshooting: If you are having trouble uploading your artwork through the Logo and Artwork screen, you can also send your artwork as an email attachment to info@greenseeker.hr.

    • If you have any questions about sending Klik4 your artwork, please call us at 01/4002233 between 9 AM and 5 PM ET or use our LiveChat feature to speak with a promotions expert.

What is a setup charge?

Setup charges are fees set by our manufacturing partners to cover the cost of setting up the printing equipment required to apply your artwork to the product.

  • Setup charges vary by product and by imprint method.
  • Each unique product in your order is subject to a setup charge?meaning that if you are customizing two separate products, you may have to pay a setup charge for each product.
  • Because we keep your art on file at our production facilities, we can typically waive the setup charge if you reorder the exact same product from us within a year.
  • If you make changes to your artwork when reordering, be aware that a new setup will have to be configured, which may result in an additional setup charge.
  • Because setup charges are designated by our manufacturing partners, they are not discounted when using a coupon code at checkout.

If you have any questions, please call 800-554-0127 or LiveChat between 9 AM and 8 PM ET to speak with a promotions expert.
